Something know about our history

In 2015 When We Start

This had been the year in which the seeds of social work had been sowed in us, we decided to celebrate our happiness
with the under privileged children during festivals and our
birthdays.

In 2017 When We Start

Our small plant (organization) was named "Dot to Drawing- from nothing to everything". Under this name all the activities conducted for the holistic development of children started being
recorded.

In 2019 When We Start

From this year more time has been allotted as well as the activities that can improve mental abilities have been organised.Students are categorised according to their skills and different batches have been assigned.

In 2016 When We Start

The seeds that had been watered at regular intervals started sprouting into our hearts and this was also recognised by our friends and we received their support in the field of social welfare services.

In 2018 When We Start

All the people who worked and supported took a decision to register the organisation as an NGO.And "Dot To Drawing Foundation" has been registered as an NGO working for the overall development of the children.
